1. Introduction

Welcome to the Training & Placement Portal of IIIT Manipur. These Terms and Conditions govern the use of our job posting services by recruiters and companies. By posting a job on our platform, you agree to comply with these terms.

2. Job Posting Guidelines

2.1 Legitimate Opportunities

  • All job postings must represent legitimate employment opportunities
  • Jobs must be for actual positions within your organization
  • No fake or misleading job postings are allowed
  • All positions must comply with applicable labor laws

2.2 Job Description Requirements

  • Job descriptions must be accurate, complete, and up-to-date
  • Compensation information must be clearly stated (salary range, benefits)
  • Job requirements must be reasonable and relevant to the position
  • No discriminatory language or requirements based on race, gender, religion, etc.

2.3 Application Process

  • You must respond to applications within a reasonable timeframe (typically 2 weeks)
  • Interview processes must be fair and transparent
  • No application fees should be charged to students
  • Provide clear feedback to candidates when possible

3. Company Verification and Profile

3.1 Company Information

  • Your company profile must be complete and verified
  • Contact information must be accurate and up-to-date
  • Company must be a legitimate business entity
  • Provide valid business registration and contact details

3.2 Verification Process

  • We may verify your company information through various means
  • You agree to provide additional documentation if requested
  • False or misleading company information may result in account suspension

4. Data Privacy and Protection

4.1 Student Data Handling

  • Student data will be handled according to applicable privacy laws
  • You agree to use student information only for recruitment purposes
  • No sharing of student data with third parties without explicit consent
  • Implement appropriate security measures to protect student data

4.2 Data Retention

  • Retain student data only for the duration necessary for recruitment
  • Delete or anonymize data when no longer needed
  • Comply with data subject rights requests

5. Prohibited Activities

  • Posting jobs for pyramid schemes or multi-level marketing
  • Requiring upfront payments or investments from candidates
  • Discriminatory hiring practices
  • Harassment or inappropriate communication with candidates
  • Using the platform for non-recruitment purposes
